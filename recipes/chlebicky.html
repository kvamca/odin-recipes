Chlebíčky

The Czech favorite dinner

Sandwiches are a guaranteed classic that will delight visiting friends or family at dinner. Try these quick sandwiches, which can be made two ways - with eggs and pollock or spread with chayote and garnished with bone-in ham.

Sandwiches with eggs and pollen: - serve 2

  • 0.5 soft bread
  • 100 g butter
  • 1 tbsp mustard
  • 3 eggs
  • lettuce pollen
  • mayonnaise
  • lemon peel grated
  • red pepper
  • parsley (onion)

Sandwiches with ham on the bone and salami: - serve 2

  • 0.5 soft bread
  • 1 salami
  • 150 g bone-in ham (finely sliced)
  • 3 eggs
  • cucumber
  • mayonnaise
  • red pepper
  • parsley (onion)

Procedure

Slice the bread and hard boil the eggs. Peel the boiled eggs, cut them into marigolds and clean and cut the red pepper into pieces.

For the egg and pollock sandwiches, mix a tablespoon of softened butter with a teaspoon of mustard (make more if needed). Spread the mixture over the sliced egg.

Lay the washed and dried lettuce on the slices and top with the egg halves cut into moons. Add a teaspoon of mayonnaise and garnish with a piece of red pepper and chopped scallions or parsley.

To prepare the ham and salami sandwiches, first spread the slices of bread with salami.

Place the ham rolled into a cone, a moon egg, a cucumber fan, mayonnaise on the tip of a knife, a piece of paprika and parsley on the coated slices.
