Bábovka

The Czech favorite breakfast

There are countless recipes for bábovka and every family has its own. Maybe several! We have a classic for you in the form of a marble cup cake - its preparation is very easy! :)

Ingredients - serve 16

  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of oil
  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 cup of milk
  • vanilla sugar
  • lemon peel grated
  • cocoa powder
  • baking powder

Procedure

Mix the sugar with the eggs, add the oil, flour, milk, baking powder, vanilla sugar and mix well.

Divide the dough into two bowls. In one bowl, add grated lemon zest (preferably organic) to taste.

Add Dutch cocoa to the other bowl with the batter, also to taste.

Pour into the greased tin and bake at 200 °C for 45-50 min.

When still hot, turn out onto a plate. Slice lukewarm.
